Non-Engine cards






Target descriptions:
  • Starter: cards that can start your combo's without help from other cards, for example Aleister The Invoker.
  • Extenders: cards that can special summon monters, for example Emergency Teleport.
  • Bomb: cards that can clear the field, for example Dark Hole.
  • Garnet: cards that you have to play but you don't want to draw them or see them in your starting hand, for example Gem-Knight Garnet.
  • Defensive: hand traps, for example Infinite Impermanence.
  • Anti-stun: cards that allow you to play the game again, for example Mystical Space Typhoon.
  • Draw: cards that allow you to go at least +1 in card advantage, for example Pot of greed.
  • Search: cards that search other cards, for example Elemental Hero Stratos.
  • Stun: cards prevent your opponent from playing the game, for example Domain of the True Monarchs.
